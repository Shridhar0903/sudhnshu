About Us

Discover our legacy, values and industrial expertise built on trust, quality and long-term customer relationships.

Company Introduction

Supplying Industry With Trust & Consistency

Established in 1974 and reformed in 1999 as Sudhanshu Associates, we are a Pune-based industrial supplier serving businesses with dependable product and dedicated service.

Our core supply range includes welding electrodes and accessories, non-destructive testing equipment, safety product, abrasive materials and power tools.

Our Journey

A Legacy Built Across Decades

1974
FOUNDATION

Our story began in 1974, in a small 150 sq. ft. space in Kasarwadi, with just 50 valued clients and a clear vision. Initially serving only the PCMC region, we laid the foundation of our business on trust, consistency, and a deep understanding of industry needs—values that continue to drive us today.

1999
RESTRUCTURED

A major turning point came in 1999, when we restructured and expanded into a 1500 sq. ft. space in Yashwant Nagar. This phase saw rapid growth, with our clientele rising to 350+ and our presence extending across Maharashtra, setting the stage for a more robust and scalable future.

Today
CURRENT

Today, we operate from a 6000 sq. ft. facility in Bhosari, serving 1000+ clients with continued growth and trust.
